Hacksaw Ridge

Synopsis:

The true story of Pfc. Desmond T. Doss (Andrew Garfield),

who won the Congressional Medal of Honor despite

refusing to bear arms during WWII on religious grounds.

Doss was drafted and ostracized by fellow soldiers for his

pacifist stance but went on to earn respect and adoration

for his bravery, selflessness and compassion after he

risked his life -- without firing a shot -- to save 75 men in the Battle of Okinawa.
